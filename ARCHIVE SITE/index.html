What We Do?

As a diversified and forward-thinking group, Jendy-Jasper Limited operates across key sectors — from natural resources to digital infrastructure — delivering value, reliability, and innovation to support Nigeria's growth and self-reliance. We begin with excellence in modern Cloud & DevOps services, expand into reliable energy solutions through our gas plant and energy services, and deliver palm oil storage and sustainable agricultural produce — creating value across digital infrastructure and natural resources

Technology

Cloud infrastructure, DevOps automation, CI/CD pipelines — bringing enterprise-grade solutions to businesses. Enterprise cloud infrastructure and DevOps services — enabling businesses to scale digitally, automate operations, and innovate securely in a connected economy.

Energy

Industrial gas processing and reliable energy supply supporting agriculture and regional development. Gas plant operations delivering processing, storage, and supply — supporting agriculture, manufacturing, and national energy infrastructure with safe, efficient solutions.

Agriculture

Palm oil storage, livestock rearing, and selected cash crops — built on sustainable Nigerian farming practices. Core foundation in palm oil storage, bulk handling, quality preservation, livestock, and cash crops — ensuring year-round supply chain reliability for food security and industrial use.

Our Businesses

From sustainable agriculture roots to reliable energy solutions and cutting-edge digital infrastructure — we deliver integrated value across natural resources and technology.

Cloud & DevOps Services

Modern technology arm offering enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure and DevOps automation — helping businesses scale securely, deploy faster, and operate efficiently in today's digital landscape.

  • Cloud infrastructure setup & management (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • CI/CD pipelines & automation (CodePipeline, Jenkins, GitHub Actions)
  • Container orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
  • Monitoring, logging & security optimization

Energy Solutions

Our industrial gas plant delivers reliable processing, storage, and supply of industrial gases. We support agriculture, manufacturing, and regional energy needs with safe, efficient operations and infrastructure services.

  • Gas processing & purification
  • Bulk storage & terminalling
  • Transportation & distribution networks
  • Engineering support for gas facilities

Agriculture & Commodities

We specialize in premium palm oil storage, seasonal bulk purchasing, quality preservation, and distribution. Our operations also include livestock management and selected cash crops — all built on sustainable Nigerian farming practices for consistent supply and long-term reliability.

  • Bulk palm oil storage (temperature-controlled)
  • Seasonal sourcing & off-season supply
  • Livestock rearing & cash crop integration
  • Quality assurance & logistics support
