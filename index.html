Who We Are

A Conglomerate
Built for Africa

Jendy Jasper Limited is a duly incorporated Nigerian enterprise operating across seven distinct business sectors. Our structure is deliberately diversified — enabling us to deploy capital, talent, and operational capacity across multiple industries simultaneously.

Registered under CAMA 2020, fully compliant with all relevant regulatory bodies, and headquartered in Imo State, we are building enterprise infrastructure that creates lasting value in the Nigerian economy.

01

CAC Incorporated

Formally registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020 with full compliance across all divisions.

02

Multi-Sector Portfolio

Seven incorporated business lines operating under one consolidated brand and governance framework.

03

Headquartered in Imo State

Physical operations, management, and decision-making rooted in Nigeria — with ambitions that extend across the continent.
